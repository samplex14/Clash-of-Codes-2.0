# Phase 1 — Rapid Fire Round

## Overview

The opening phase of Clash of Codes, open to **all registered participants** across both tracks.

## Format

- All participants compete together simultaneously.
- A series of **quick questions** are thrown at players under strict time pressure.
- Questions are fast-paced and designed to test both **speed** and **accuracy**.

## Qualification

- Only the **top 64 scorers** from this round advance to Phase 2.
- Rankings are determined by score; tiebreakers (if any) are applied based on completion time.

## Key Points

| Detail     | Value                          |
| ---------- | ------------------------------ |
| Open to    | All registered participants    |
| Style      | Rapid-fire Q&A                 |
| Qualifiers | Top 64                         |
| Tracks     | 1st Year & 2nd Year (separate) |

## Next Phase

Top 64 qualifiers move on to [Phase 2 — MCQ Duels](./phase-2-mcq-duels.md).
