# Clash of Codes 2.0 - Official Rules
## 1. General Guidelines

- **Event Tracks**: The competition is divided into two tracks: **1st Year** and **2nd Year**.
- **Fair Play**: Any attempt to manipulate network traffic, exploit API endpoints, or use automated scripts will result in immediate disqualification.
- **Device Usage**: Please use a single device/browser tab for the entire duration of the event to ensure state consistency.

## 2. The Arena & Matchmaking

- **Waiting Room**: Once registered, you will enter the Arena. You must remain on this page until matchmaking is completed.
- **Opponents**: You will be paired with another participant from your track.
- **Do Not Refresh**: Avoid refreshing the page while matchmaking is in progress.

## 3. Phase 1: The Code Battle

- **Format**: A 1-vs-1 independent quiz battle. While you have an opponent, your score is ranked globally.
- **Time Limit**: You have **30 minutes** to complete the challenge.
- **Questions**:
  - Questions are shuffled uniquely for every participant.
  - All questions are multiple-choice.
- **Submission Rules**:
  - **Manual Submission**: You can only manually submit your battle if you have answered **ALL** questions. Partial manual submissions are not allowed.
  - **Auto-Submission**: If the timer reaches **00:00**, the system will automatically submit whatever answers you have **confirmed** so far. Unanswered questions will receive zero points.
- **Scoring**:
  - Points are awarded for correct answers.
  - There is no negative marking (unless specified otherwise by event admins).

## 4. Leaderboard & Qualification

- **Ranking Criteria**:
  1.  **Score**: Higher score is better.
  2.  **Time**: If scores are tied, the participant who submitted **earlier** is ranked higher.
- **Qualification**: The **Top 16** participants from each track will qualify for the next round.
- **Status**: The leaderboard is the final source of truth for qualification status.
- **Manual Refresh**: The leaderboard does not auto-update. You must refresh the page manually to see the latest rankings.

## 5. Technical Constraints

- **Session State**: Your progress is saved locally. Clearing your browser cache or switching devices mid-game may result in data loss.
- **Disconnection**: If you get disconnected, reload the page immediately. The system attempts to restore your session, but time lost is not compensated.
